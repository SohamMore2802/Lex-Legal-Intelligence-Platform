Frequently Asked Questions

What file types are supported?

Currently, Lex AI supports digital PDF files only. Scanned images are not yet supported.

How does answering work?

The app extracts text from the PDF, breaks it into chunks, and finds the best matching passages for your query.

Is my document stored?

Uploaded files are saved locally to the `data/uploads` folder for processing. The app does not push your document to any external service.
